About Warner Bros

Warner Bros is a renowned entertainment company known for producing and distributing a wide range of movies, TV shows, and games. With a rich history dating back to its founding in 1923, Warner Bros has become a major player in the entertainment industry.

The company has been responsible for iconic films and franchises, including the Harry Potter series, The Dark Knight Trilogy, and DC Comics adaptations. In addition to movies, Warner Bros has produced popular TV shows such as Friends, Game of Thrones, and The Big Bang Theory.

Warner Bros Interactive Entertainment, a division of the company, focuses on creating and publishing high-quality video games. They have developed notable games like Batman: Arkham series, Middle-earth: Shadow of War, and Injustice 2.

With a commitment to delivering exceptional entertainment experiences, Warner Bros continues to captivate audiences worldwide through its diverse range of content.
